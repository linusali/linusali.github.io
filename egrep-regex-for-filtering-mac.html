egrep Regex for filtering mac

Published: Thu 18 January 2018
By MS

In Tip & Tricks.

egrep Regex

'([0-9a-f]{2}:){5}[0-9a-f]{2}'

Example

for i in $(virsh list |grep -v Name|awk '{print $2}' ) ; do 
   echo -e $i 
   virsh domiflist $i
done | egrep '([0-9a-f]{2}:){5}[0-9a-f]{2}' | awk '{print $NF}'
